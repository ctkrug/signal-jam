    A daily puzzle game you play in the browser

    Signal Jam is a free daily puzzle game that runs entirely in your browser, with no login and nothing to download. Each day there is one new puzzle: a single hidden radio signal buried in a band of noise alongside five decoys doing their best to look like it. You sweep a spectrum analyzer across the band, read the clue each decoy leaves behind, and try to lock onto the real signal before your four sweeps run out. Everyone plays the same board on the same day.

    How the spectrum puzzle works

    Every emitter on the band has three properties: a frequency, a duty cycle, and a noise floor. Decoys copy some of the real signal's properties but never all of them. Cross a decoy and it costs you a sweep, but it also reveals the one property that gives it away, so the round is deduction rather than blind guessing. Cross the real signal and it flares, snaps to its exact frequency, and chimes. Win or lose, you get a Wordle-style result grid that shows how the round went without revealing the day's answer, so you can share it without spoilers.

    Frequently asked questions

    Is Signal Jam a daily puzzle game I can play in the browser?
    Yes. It is a browser puzzle game with a new puzzle every day. Nothing to install and no account to create: open the page and start sweeping.
    How is this a Wordle-style spectrum puzzle?
    Like Wordle, everyone gets the same puzzle each day, you have a limited number of guesses, and you share a spoiler-free emoji grid of your result. The twist is that your guesses are sweeps across a spectrum analyzer, and the feedback is a live waterfall display instead of colored letters.
    Do I need to know anything about radios or spectrum analyzers?
    No. The puzzle teaches its own three properties (frequency, duty cycle, and noise floor) as you play, the same way Wordle teaches its color feedback. Prior radio knowledge is not required.
    Is there a new puzzle every day?
    Yes. The board is generated from the date, so a fresh signal, decoy set, and sweep budget arrive every day at midnight UTC, and there is a countdown to the next one once you finish.
    Does it cost anything or need an account?
    No. Signal Jam is free, has no accounts, and has no backend. Your streak and daily result are stored only in your own browser.
